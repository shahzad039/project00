  • Working with team on Project:Child Labor in Pakistan

    Our research paper on child labor in Pakistan utilized surveys, interviews, and secondary data analisis to explore the extent and causes of the pervasive issues.Poverty and lack of Education were found to be major drivers of child labor,particularly in the informal sector.
